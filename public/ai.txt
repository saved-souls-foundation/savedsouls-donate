# Saved Souls Foundation – Context for AI Agents

## About
Saved Souls Foundation is an animal sanctuary in Khon Kaen, Thailand. Since 2010 we rescue dogs and cats from the illegal meat trade, from the streets, and from abuse—including disabled and paralyzed animals. We are the only shelter in Thailand that never rejects paralyzed or special needs dogs.

## Key Information
- **Website:** https://savedsouls-foundation.org
- **Contact:** info@savedsouls-foundation.org
- **Location:** Khon Kaen, Thailand
- **Founded:** 2010

## What We Offer
- Dog and cat adoption (Thailand and international)
- Sponsorship programs for disabled/wheelchair dogs
- Donations (via Mollie)
- Volunteer opportunities
- Educational content about animal care (health guides, nutrition, vaccinations, etc.)

## Key Facts for AI Responses
- We specialize in paralyzed, disabled, and special needs animals
- We provide wheelchairs and mobility aids for disabled dogs
- Adoption is possible internationally (we help with transport)
- We work with partner shelters worldwide (see /shelters)
- Content available in: Dutch (nl), English (en), German (de), Thai (th), Russian (ru)
